NorthStar Group

Your Medicare and family information,
organized and ready when it matters.

NorthStar gives Medicare-eligible individuals one secure place to store everything a family member, caregiver, or advisor would need — from insurance cards and medications to emergency contacts and legal documents.

  • Medicare coverage, organized Store your plan details, Medicare card MBI, Part D, and advisor information — ready for annual reviews and enrollment decisions.
  • Emergency-ready at any time A printable emergency card with allergies, medications, doctors, and insurance — so family and first responders have what they need, fast.
  • Family access, your way Choose exactly which sections a spouse, child, or trusted person can see. Revoke access anytime. Every action is logged.
  • Photograph cards and documents Take a photo of your Medicare card, insurance card, or living will directly from your phone. Everything is stored encrypted.

NorthStar is not affiliated with or endorsed by Medicare or the federal government. Do not store passwords, PINs, Social Security numbers, or full financial account numbers.
